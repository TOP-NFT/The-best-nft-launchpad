Reveal Mechanics

Control how and when holders discover what's inside their packs.

⚡

Instant Reveal

Packs reveal immediately when opened. NFT metadata is live from mint day.

  • ✓ Immediate gratification
  • ✓ No waiting period
  • ✓ Simpler smart contract
⏱️

Timed Reveal

All packs reveal simultaneously on a specific date. Builds massive anticipation.

  • ✓ Community event
  • ✓ Hype & FOMO marketing
  • ✓ Fair distribution
🔢

Sequential Reveal

Each pack reveals 24–48h after purchase. Staggered discovery keeps interest alive.

  • ✓ Extended engagement
  • ✓ Organic social moments
  • ✓ No gas wars
🗝️

Holder-Triggered

Holders choose when to reveal. Burn the pack NFT to unlock the contents.

  • ✓ Holder controls timing
  • ✓ Burn creates scarcity
  • ✓ Pack NFTs have own value
