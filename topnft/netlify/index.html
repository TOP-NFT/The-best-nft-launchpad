Built For Creators

🔐

OG & Whitelist Phases

Reward your earliest supporters with exclusive early access at lower prices, powered by cryptographic Merkle tree allowlists.

🌐

Permanent IPFS Storage

Your images and metadata are stored on IPFS via NFT.Storage — decentralised, permanent, and censorship-resistant.

⚡

No Code Required

Upload images, fill the form, click deploy. No terminal, no Rust, no Anchor — just a browser and a Phantom wallet.

💰

On-Chain Royalties

Set your royalty percentage at deploy time. Every secondary sale on any marketplace pays your wallet automatically.

🎯

Free Mint Support

Set any phase price to 0 SOL for a free mint. Full control over which phases are free and which are paid.

🔗

Shareable Mint Page

Every collection gets a dedicated mint page URL your community can bookmark, share on Twitter, and return to mint from.
